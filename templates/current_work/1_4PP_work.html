In this project, we have modified an existing Four-Point Probe method for extracting the contact resistivity of an interface. Original method required additional test samples and time-consuming numerical analysis. We have replaced the latter with an analytical formulation which helped reduce the need for time-consuming numerical analysis and also provided a way to exclude the need for extra test sample.
To verify the validity of the proposed method, we compared the results with numerical simulations (which resulted in an excellent relative error lower than ~2%), followed by the comparing experimental results extracted using standard methods. Moreover, we implemented a free numerical tool that calculates the contact resistivity based on the user parameters.
